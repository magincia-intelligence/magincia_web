## Top of the brief
- **Clare: 2025 Graduate Outcomes Survey shows 75.4% undergraduate full-time employment and growing VET–university crossover (26 June).** The Department of Education released the 2025 Graduate Outcomes Survey on 26 June, after Friday's brief was filed. Undergraduate full-time employment within four to six months of completion rose to 75.4%, up from 74% in 2024. The release also highlighted growing movement between university and vocational pathways, with Clare describing the data as evidence that "the barriers between university and vocational education continue to come down." The GOS figures land one day before ATEC takes on responsibility for overseeing the Managed Growth Funding System and needs-based funding, adding an employment-outcomes baseline against which the new commission's equity mandate will be measured.
