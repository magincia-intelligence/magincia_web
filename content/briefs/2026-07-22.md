## Top of the brief

- **UK cabinet reshuffle installs Lucy Powell as Education Secretary; the policy direction of the levy on international student fee income under the new Burnham government is uncertain (21 July).** Powell replaces Bridget Phillipson, who had pressed ahead with the levy on English universities' international student fee income despite widespread sector objections. New Home Secretary Mahmood has already halted study visas from four countries on asylum grounds. The PIE notes it "remains to be seen" whether Burnham's administration will support international education given "rising anti-immigration sentiment" — a trajectory that operates alongside Australia's competing position under a 295,000 NPL and $2,500 visa fee. ([The PIE, 21 July](https://thepienews.com/new-uk-education-secretary-amid-burnhams-first-cabinet-reshuffle/))
